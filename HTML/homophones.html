LES HOMOPHONES

These are words that sound the same bit have different meanings.

Here are some of the common french homophones:

  1. Mer - Sea / Mère - Mother
  2. Vers - Towards / Verre - Glass / Vert - Green
  3. Ça - That / Sa - His/her
  4. La - The / Là - There
  5. Dents - Teeth / Dans - In
  6. Foi - Faith / Foie - Liver / Fois - Times
  7. Bal - A dance:event / Balle - Ball
  8. Ou - Or / Où - where
  9. Peu - Few / Peut - Can
