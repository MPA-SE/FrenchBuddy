LES PARONYMES

These are words that look and sound similar but have different meanings.

Here are some of the common french paronyms:

  1. Abjurer - To recant / Adjurer - To beg
  2. Emmener - To take somebody or something / Amener - To bring somebody or something
  3. Emménager - To move in / Aménager - To arrange
  4. Éminent - Distinguished / Imminent - About to happen
  5. Amoral - Without morals / Immoral - Morally wrong
  6. Prodige - Prodigy / Prodigue - Lavish
  7. Crier - To scream / Créer - To create
  8. Invoquer - To call upon / Évoquer - To evoke
  9. Lier - To link / Nier - To deny
  10. Accepter - To accept / Excepté - Besides:conjunction
