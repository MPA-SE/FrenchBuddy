FAUX AMIS (FALSE FRIENDS)

These are words that look similar to English words but have different meanings.

Note that the f means that the word is feminine and the m means that the word is masculine. A word can be both depending on the use. The pl stands for plural.

Here is a list of these faux amis:

  1. Actuellement - Currently (NOT actually, which is = réllement, en fait)
  2. Accomodation f - Adjustment or adaptation (NOT accomodation, which is = logement m)
  3. An m - year (NOT "an", which is = un, une)
  4. Appointements m/pl - Salary or wage (NOT appointments, which is = des rendez-vous)
  5. As m/pl - Aces (NOT "as", which is = comme)
  6. Attendre - To wait (NOT to attend)
  7. Assister à- To attend (NOT to assist)
  8. Avertissement m - Warning (NOT advertisement, which is = annonce f, publicité) f
  9. Basket m - Basketball (NOT basket, which is = panier m)
  10. Bail m - lease or rent (NOT bail)
  11. Bouclette f - Curl (NOT booklet, which is = livret m , carnet m)
  12. Bulle f - Bubble (NOT bull, which is = taureau m)
  13. But m - Goal (NOT "but", which is = mais)
  14. Bras m - Arm (NOT bra, which is = soutien gorge m)
  15. Bribe f - Snippet (NOT bribe, which is = pot de vin m)
  16. Blesser - To hurt (NOT to bless, which is = bénir)
  17. Caution f - Bail (NOT caution, which is = prudence f)
  18. Car - Because: conjunction/coach/bus (NOT car, which is = voiture f)
  19. Casquette f - Cap (NOT casket, which is = coffre m)
  20. Coin m- Corner (NOT coin, which is = monnaie f)
  21. Court - Short (NOT court, which is = tribunal m)
  22. Comment - How (NOT comment, which is = commentaire m)
  23. Concurrence f - Competition, rivalry (NOT concurrence, which is = assentiment m)
  24. Course f - Race/racing (NOT course = cours m)
  25. Chair f - Flesh (NOT chair, which is = chaise f)
  26. Désagrément m - inconvenience (NOT disagreement, which is = désaccord m)
  27. Déception f - Disappointment (NOT deception, which is = tromperie f)
  28. Décevoir - To disappoint (NOT to deceive, which is = tromper)
  29. Devise f - Currency, motto (NOT device, which is = appareil m)
  30. Évent m - blowhole, fountain (NOT event, which is = Évenement m)
  31. Éventuellement - Possibly (NOT eventually, which is = finalement , enfin)
  32. Fabrique f - Factory (NOT fabric, which is = tissu m)
  33. Foot m - Football/soccer (NOT foot, which is = pied m)
  34. Fin f - End/thin:adjective (NOT fin, which is = nageoire f)
  35. Journée f - Day (NOT journey, which is = voyage m)
  36. Librairie f - Bookshop (NOT library, which is = bibliothèque f)
  37. Lecture f - Reading (NOT lecture, which is = cours m)
  38. Rester - To stay/remain (NOT to rest, which is = reposer)
  39. Sensible - Sensitive (NOT sensible, which is = raisonnable, prudent)
  40. Travailler - To work (NOT to travel, which is = voyager)
  41. Grappe f - Bunch (NOT grape, which is = raisin m)
  42. Grave - Serious (NOT grave/tomb)
  43. Habit m - Outfit (NOT habit)
  44. Haïr - To hate (NOT hair)
  45. Hasard m - Chance/coincidence (NOT hazard)
  46. Injurier - to insult (NOT to injure)
  47. Joli - Pretty (NOT jolly)
  48. Location f - Rental/hire (NOT location)
  49. Main f - Hand (NOT main)
  50. Manger - The verb "to eat" (NOT MANGER)
  51. Magasin m - Store/shop (NOT magazine)
  52. Médecin m - Doctor (NOT medicine)
  53. Or m - gold/yet:conjunction (NOT or)
  54. Pain m - Bread (NOT pain)
  55. Partir - To leave (NOT to party)
  56. Parole f - Speech/word (NOT parole)
  57. Passer un examen - To take an exam (NOT to pass an exam)
  58. Patron m - Boss (NOT patron)
  59. Photographe m/f - Photographer (NOT photograph)
  60. Préservatif m - Condom (NOT preservative)
  61. Procès m - trial/lawsuit (NOT process)
  62. Propre - Clean (NOT proper)
  63. Prier - To pray (NOT to pry)
  64. Plainte f - Complaint/grievance (NOT plant)
  65. Race f - Breed (NOT race: that is, racing)
  66. Relever - To raise (NOT to relieve)
  67. Rentable - profitable or cost-effective (NOT RENTABLE)
  68. Regarder - To watch/look at (NOT to regard)
  69. Résumer - To summarise (NOT to resume)
  70. Retirer - To withdraw (NOT to retire)
  71. Roman m - Novel (NOT a roman)
  72. Sale - Dirty (NOT sale)
  73. Sentence f - Sentence at a tribunal (NOT sentence)
  74. Slip m - Briefs/underwear (NOT slip)
  75. Store m - Blind/shade (NOT blind person)
  76. Stage m - Internship (NOT stage)
  77. Surveiller - To supervise (NOT to survey)
  78. Supplier - To beg/plead (NOT to supply)
  79. Surnom m - Nickname (NOT surname)
  80. Subir - To undergo (NOT subdue)
  81. Tentative f - Attempt (NOT tentative)
  82. Tong f - Flip-flop (NOT tongue)
  83. Vacances pl/f - Holidays (NOT vacancies)
  84. Veste f - Jacket (NOT vest)
  85. Venue f - Coming/Arrival (NOT venue)
  86. Quitter - To leave (NOT to quit)
