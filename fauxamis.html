FAUX AMIS (FALSE FRIENDS)

These are words that look similar to English words but have different meanings.

Here is a list of these faux amis:

  1. Actuellement - Currently (NOT actually)
  2. Attendre - To wait (NOT to attend)
  3. Assister à- To attend (NOT to assist)
  4. Avertissement m - Warning (NOT advertisement)
  5. Basket m - Basketball (NOT basket)
  6. Bras m - Arm (NOT bra)
  7. Bribe f - Snippet (NOT bribe)
  8. Blesser - To hurt (NOT to bless)
  9. Caution f - Bail (NOT caution)
  10. Car - Because: conjunction/coach/bus (NOT car)
  11. Course f - Race/racing (NOT course)
  12. Chair f - Flesh (NOT chair)
  13. Concurrence f - Competition (NOT concurrence)
  14. Demander - To ask (NOT to demand)
  15. Éventuellement - Possibly (NOT eventually)
  16. Envie f - Desire/wish (NOT envy)
  17. Fabrique f - Factory (NOT fabric)
  18. Foot m - Football/soccer (NOT foot)
  19. Fin f - End/thin:adjective (NOT fin)
  20. Gentil - Nice/kind (NOT gentle)
  21. Journée f - Day (NOT journey)
  22. Librairie f - Bookshop (NOT library)
  23. Lecture f - Reading (NOT lecture)
  24. Rester - To stay/remain (NOT to rest)
  25. Sensible - Sensitive (NOT sensible)
  26. Travailler - To work (NOT to travel)
  27. Coin m- Corner (NOT coin/money)
  28. Court - Short (NOT court)
  29. Déception f - Disappointment (NOT deception)
  30. Décevoir - To disappoint (NOT to deceive)
  31. Douche f - Shower (NOT douche)
  32. Grappe f - Bunch (NOT grape)
  33. Grave - Serious (NOT grave/tomb)
  34. Habit m - Outfit (NOT habit)
  35. Haïr - To hate (NOT hair)
  36. Hasard m - Chance/coincidence (NOT hazard)
  37. Injurier - to insult (NOT to injure)
  38. Joli - Pretty (NOT jolly)
  39. Location f - Rental/hire (NOT location)
  40. Main f - Hand (NOT main)
  41. Magasin m - Store/shop (NOT magazine)
  42. Médecin m - Doctor (NOT medicine)
  43. Or m - gold/yet:conjunction (NOT or)
  44. Pain m - Bread (NOT pain)
  45. Partir - To leave (NOT to party)
  46. Parole f - Speech/word (NOT parole)
  47. Passer un examen - To take an exam (NOT to pass an exam)
  48. Patron m - Boss (NOT patron)
  49. Photographe m/f - Photographer (NOT photograph)
  50. Préservatif m - Condom (NOT preservative)
  51. Procès m - trial/lawsuit (NOT process)
  52. Propre - Clean (NOT proper)
  53. Prier - To pray (NOT to pry)
  54. Plainte f - Complaint/grievance (NOT plant)
  55. Race f - Breed (NOT race: that is, racing)
  56. Relever - To raise (NOT to relieve)
  57. Regarder - To watch/look at (NOT to regard)
  58. Résumer - To summarise (NOT to resume)
  59. Retirer - To withdraw (NOT to retire)
  60. Roman m - Novel (NOT a roman)
  61. Sale - Dirty (NOT sale)
  62. Sentence f - Sentence at a tribunal (NOT sentence)
  63. Slip m - Briefs/underwear (NOT slip)
  64. Store m - Blind/shade (NOT blind person)
  65. Stage m - Internship (NOT stage)
  66. Surveiller - To supervise (NOT to survey)
  67. Supplier - To beg/plead (NOT to supply)
  68. Surnom m - Nickname (NOT surname)
  69. Subir - To undergo (NOT subdue)
  70. Tentative f - Attempt (NOT tentative)
  71. Tong f - Flip-flop (NOT tongue)
  72. Vacances pl/f - Holidays (NOT vacancies)
  73. Veste f - Jacket (NOT vest)
  74. Venue f - Coming/Arrival (NOT venue)
  75. Quitter - To leave (NOT to quit)

Note that the f means that the word is feminine and the m means that the word is masculine. A word can be both depending on the use. The pl stands for plural.
