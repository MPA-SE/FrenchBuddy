What does FrenchBuddy Offer?

There are many reasons to choose FrenchBuddy as your language learning platform. Here are just a few:

  • Learn FAUX AMIS: Learn words that look similar to English but have different meanings.
  • Learn French Homophones: Learn to differentiate between words that sound the same but have different meanings .
  • Learn French Paronyms: Learn to differentiate words that look and sound alike but have different meanings. .
  • Learn French Heterophones: Learn words that are spelt the same but are pronounced differently.
  • Learn LES ANGLICISMES: Learn words that are adopted from English, with altered meanings.
  • Learn French Pronominal Verbs: Learn verbs that use reflexive pronouns.
  • Learn French Idiomatic Expressions: Learn phrases that do not translate literally.
  • Learn Verlan: Learn French slang.
