LES VERBES PRONOMINAUX

Verbs that use reflexive pronouns (se, me, te, etc.):

  1. Se lever - To get up
  2. Se laver - To wash oneself
  3. Se promener -To take a walk
  4. S'amuser - To have fun
  5. Se reposer - To rest
  6. S'ennuyer - To get bored
  7. Se réveiller - To wake up
  8. Se coucher - To go to bed
  9. Se doucher -To shower
  10. Se préparer- To get ready
