LES ANGLICISMES

These are French words adopted from English, but sometimes have altered meanings

Here are some of the common anglicisms:

  1. Parking - Car park, not "parking"
  2. Shampooing - Shampoo, not "shampooing"
  3. Brushing - Blow drying, not "brushing"
  4. Baskets - Sneakers, not "baskets"
  5. Smoking - Tuxedo, not "smoking"
  6. Parking - Car park, not "parking"
